Debt recycling is a strategy that helps Australian homeowners turn their non-deductible mortgage debt into tax-deductible investment debt. By making extra home loan repayments and reborrowing that equity to invest in income-producing assets like shares or managed funds, the interest on your investment loan may become tax-deductible. Dividends and tax savings are then used to pay down your mortgage faster, building wealth and reducing your tax at the same time.
